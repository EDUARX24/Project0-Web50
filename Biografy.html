SEBASTIAN VETTEL

Sebastian Vettel was born on July 3, 1987 in Heppenheim, Germany. From a very young age, he showed great talent for motorsport and started racing karting at the age of 8. In the following years, Vettel excelled in various grassroots motorsport categories and in 2006 made his Formula 1 debut with the BMW Sauber team.

In 2007 Vettel joined the Toro Rosso team and in 2008 he took his first victory at the Italian Grand Prix, becoming the youngest driver to win a Formula 1 race. Vettel joined the Red Bull Racing team in 2009 and started a highly successful partnership with the team. During his time with Red Bull, Vettel won four consecutive Formula 1 championships, from 2010 to 2013, setting several records in the process.

In 2015, Vettel joined the Ferrari team, but failed to replicate his previous success. Despite this, he remained a respected driver and admired for his skill and positive attitude on and off the track. In 2021, Vettel joined the Aston Martin team (previously known as Racing Point) and announced his retirement from Formula 1 at the end of the season. In his career, Vettel achieved 53 Formula 1 race victories and was highly respected for his skill and positive attitude. Off the track, he is passionate about music and plays various instruments.
